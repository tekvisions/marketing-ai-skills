# Guardrails & Restrictions
- Do not accept software-based tracking (like Google Analytics) as the final word on source attribution.
- Never make self-reported attribution "How did you hear about us?" a simple dropdown menu; use free text.
- Avoid under-investing in podcasts and communities simply because they lack direct ROAS visibility.
